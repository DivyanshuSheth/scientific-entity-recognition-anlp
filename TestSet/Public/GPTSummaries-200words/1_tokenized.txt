This research paper explores various methods and systems for different natural language processing tasks . The baseline and proposed systems / methods mentioned in the paper include BERT , sequence tagging architectures , graph - induction based frame extraction , and GPT2 - base . Hyperparameters mentioned include L , H , SVS - m , KS - m , and λ . Evaluation metrics used for method evaluation are model performance measures like F1 score , BLEU score , topic coherence metrics , and Kripendorff 's α . The paper is evaluated on tasks such as entity typing , sentiment analysis , summarization , prompt learning , text classification , etc . The datasets used in these tasks include GLUE , Yelp , TourSG , StanfordLU , etc . The paper provides evaluation results on these metrics for each method .
